Airfryer Coconut Shrimp Recipe!

This recipe is super simple to create with relatively little mess created for easy clean up! It will take approximately an hour with the majority of the time needing to prep the shrimp and ingredients. The only kitchen appliance you will need is your airfryer which makes everything super simple so follow along!

Ingreadients:

  • 1/2 Cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 Teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 2/3 Cup unsweetened flaked coconut
  • 1/3 Cup panko bread crumbs
  • 12 Oz of uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Cooking spray
  • 1/2 Tespoon kosher salt, divided
  • 1/4 Cup honey
  • 1/4 Cup lime juice

Steps:

  1. Stir flour and pepper in one dish, beat eggs in another dish, and stir coconut and panko in a third dish.
  2. One at a time, dredge shrimp in flour mixture, then in egg mixture, and then in coconut mixture.
  3. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees F, then place shrimp inside and cook for 3 minutes, turn it over, and cook again for 3 minutes.
